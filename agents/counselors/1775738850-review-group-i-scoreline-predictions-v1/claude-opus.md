- 2022: Qatar (hosts) — 1 goal in 3 matches

- 2014: Honduras — 1 goal, Cameroon — 1 goal

**Fix:** Change France vs Iraq to **3-1**. This is the most natural spot — France up 2-0 or 3-0, Iraq get a late consolation, Deschamps rotates. ## 6. Model Divergences

You deviate from the modal 1-1 on 5 of 6 matches. The model produces 1-1 as modal for all 6 matches, which is a known artifact of Poisson models with λ values near 1.0-1.8 — the modal scoreline tends to be 1-1 even when the distribution is skewed. **Your deviations are justified.** Picking 1-1 for all six would be the actual mistake.

The one divergence I'd push back on: **France vs Iraq 3-0 vs model 2-0.** Going above the modal is fine, but doing so while also giving Iraq 0 goals creates the shutout problem. 3-1 or 2-1 would be better choices that still capture France's dominance.

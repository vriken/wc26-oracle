# Second Opinion: Group A Scoreline Predictions

## 1. Are the scorelines plausible?

**But collectively they tell a story that's too clean.** Every match is decided. Zero draws in six matches. The base rate for draws in World Cup group stages is ~25%. Six matches with zero draws has a probability of roughly 0.75^6 ≈ 18%. Not impossible, but you're betting against the base rate for no clear reason.

## 2. Is 12 goals too low? Yes.

This is the biggest problem. You're predicting 2.00 g/m for Group A while your own tournament prediction implies 2.73 g/m. That's a 27% shortfall.

Your justification — "tightest group, no extreme mismatches" — is valid directionally. This group *should* be below average. But not this far below. For context:

- **Manager:** Thomas Tuchel (appointed late 2024, contract extended through Euro 2028)

## 6. Cape Verde (Cabo Verde) -- WORLD CUP DEBUTANTS

### Manager & Tactics
- **Manager:** Pedro "Bubista" Brito (former Cape Verde international defender, in charge since 2020)
  - 48% win rate. Taken Cape Verde to three major tournaments including two AFCON quarter-finals
- **Formation:** 4-2-3-1 (primary) / 4-3-3 (alternative)
- **Style:** Compact, defense-first, but increasingly proactive. Press with intent, win ball in useful areas, attack in transition before opposition organizes. Set-piece threat. Bubista has pushed the team "beyond the old underdog mindset of simply sitting deep."

### Qualifying Record (CAF Group D -- 1st place, direct qualification)

Finished 4 points clear of Cameroon. Group also included Angola, Libya, Eswatini, Mauritius.

**Key qualifying results:**
- Cape Verde 3-0 Eswatini (clinched qualification, Oct 13, 2025 -- Livramento, Semedo, Stopira scored)
- Libya 3-3 Cape Verde (dramatic draw, missed chance to clinch earlier)
- Cameroon 4-1 Cape Verde (only defeat, Jun 2024)

### Historic Achievement
- First ever World Cup qualification for Cape Verde
- Population: ~525,000 (10 islands, 9 inhabited)
- Smallest country by land area and second-least populated (after Iceland) to qualify for a World Cup
- Third debutant at 2026 WC alongside Uzbekistan and Jordan
- 22nd team to qualify overall

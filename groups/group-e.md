**Changes from v1:**

**Impact:**
- Total goals: 13 → 15 (2.17 → 2.50 g/m) — closer to WC average and model expectation (14.2)
- Draws: 1 → 2 (17% → 33%) — closer to 25% base rate

**Tiebreaker fix:** v1 claimed Ecuador finished 2nd over Ivory Coast, but with v1 picks both were on 4pts, +1 GD, 1-1 H2H, and CIV had 4 GF vs ECU's 3 GF — so CIV should have been 2nd under FIFA's goals-scored tiebreaker, not ECU. v2 fixes this structural error by giving Ecuador 5pts.

**Counselors consensus:** 3 independent reviewers (Claude Opus 4, Gemini 2.0 Flash Thinking, o1) all agreed on both changes. Key insight: v1 was too aggressive in overriding the model without strong justification, especially on low-probability outcomes (Curacao 0 goals, Ecuador 0 goals vs Germany).
